# MLFINA Blueprint

MLFINA is a team for people who want to do serious work in quantamental investment. This blueprint is here to remove ambiguity. If you work with us, you should know what we value, how we move, and what we expect from one another.

## 1. We gather around the same vision.

We are here because we believe in high-quality research, high-quality investment work, and a high standard for how we live and work. This is not a loose affiliation. It is a group built around ambition, craft, and shared direction.

## 2. We use AI across the full research stack.

We use AI in idea generation, literature review, coding, data work, writing, replication, communication, and operations. We do not use AI as a substitute for judgment. We use it as leverage to think better, move faster, and operate at a higher level.

## 3. We value first-principle thinking.

We do not accept ideas, methods, or traditions just because they are common. We start from the underlying problem, the economics, the data, and the constraints. We want people who can reason from the ground up instead of copying what already exists.

## 4. We build complete shit early, then iterate.

In the early stage of research, the goal is not elegance. The goal is to get to a complete first version fast. We do not try to build a masterpiece in the first iteration. We build something crude but functional, learn from it, and improve from there.

## 5. We back each other and trust each other.

Trust is the default. We assume good intent, we share context, and we help each other win. If one person gets stronger, the team gets stronger.

## 6. We want things faster. Always faster.

Speed matters. Research compounds when cycles are short. We value people who can reduce turnaround time without lowering standards, and we treat unnecessary delay as a real cost.

## 7. We value direct communication over hierarchy.

We do not hide behind titles, org charts, or indirect signals. If something matters, say it clearly to the right person. Clarity is respect.

## 8. We value action over deliberation.

Thinking matters, but endless discussion is usually a tax on progress. When the path is reasonably clear, we act. We would rather learn from reality than from another meeting.

## 9. We value asynchronous communication.

Good written communication scales better than constant meetings. Write things down, make decisions legible, and give people room to think deeply. Meetings are for what cannot be handled well in writing.

## 10. Disagreement should be visible.

You should know when someone disagrees with you, and they should know when you disagree with them. Hidden disagreement creates fake alignment. Real disagreement, surfaced early and handled directly, makes the work better.

That is the standard.

We are here to do excellent work, move quickly, trust one another, and build a team that is serious about research, investment, and life.
